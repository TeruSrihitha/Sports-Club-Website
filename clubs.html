SPORTSCLUBS

BASKETBALL

Basketball is a team sport where two teams,usually having five players on each team,play against each other on a rectangular court.The objective is to get the ball through a hoop mounted high on a backboard on the opponent's side of the court,while preventing the opponent from shootig it into your team's hoop.

FOOTBALL

Football is one of the most exciting games played world over. The rules of the game were almost the same every where. It is played between two teams with 11 players in each team.It is played with a big circular ball which is kicked by the players with the aim to hit the goal. It takes a lot of energy and enthusiasm to win the game.

VOLLEYBALL

Volleball is played with a ball and a net,with teams on each side of net.One team hits the ball over the net and into the other team's court,the other team must then hit the ball back over the net and in bounds within 3 tries without letting the ball touch the ground. "Team volleyball",(indoors)with 12 (6 per team) & Beach volleyball (outdoors) with 2 per team.

HANDBALL

Handball combines the elements of soccer and basketball,as six players move the ball down a floor that is larger than a basket ball court and try to score by throwing the ball past a goal keeper into the set.A successful scoring attempt results in the award of a single point.Typical final scores in this action-packed game run in the mid twenties.

SWIMMING

There are four strokes in competetive swimming.The freestyle has no restrictions.The three other style are the backstroke, the breaststroke which is commonly called as frog style and the butterfly.Freestyle races are majorly over 50m,100m,200m,400m and extra 1500m for men & extra 800m for women.

CRICKET

Cricket,a bat-and-ball game adjudicated by 2 umpires played between 2 teams of 11 players on a field at the centre of which is 22-yard pitch and a wicket at each end, each having 2 bails balanced on 3 stumps.Batters score runs by striking the ball bowled at the wicket with the bat,but bowling,fielding side tries prevent this (so they are "out").
